# Take Controller - Helix Stadium MIDI Messager
---

AI made this. I just had a need. Not a need for speed, although I recently DID just complete a speed awareness course. My mate said it's probably because I'm a speed demon, and that speed demons can never change. He was a bit smug about it. Anyway. That same mate, he wanted to queue up songs for me instead of me doing it all on the Line 6 Helix Stadium and doing a merry dance. Tbh I'd rather he took the blame than me.

It Just Works. Plug in a USB-C to MIDI dongle to your iPad, and violá.
